Assumptions and rules
Standard Ultimate Texas Hold'em: Ante and Blind are both 1 unit. The dealer opens with a pair or better. Ante pushes if the dealer does not open. Play pays 1:1. Blind pays according to the editable paytable above only on player wins. Trips side bet is not included.

Known dealer cards are used directly. If one dealer card is known, only the second card is enumerated/simulated. Other players' cards are treated as dead cards and removed from the deck; they are not competing hands.

Exact EV is used when the board has 3, 4, or 5 cards, subject to the exact enumeration limit. Pre-flop has billions of possible outcomes, so the action uses the common optimal/basic 4x chart and the odds display uses simulation.
